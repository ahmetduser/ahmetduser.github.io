About Group Work and Diversity

Posted by Ahmet Duser  •  July 24, 2020

I have lived in Dubai for more than 2 years and the diversity Dubai holds enabled me to involve myself with many cultures at once and in Dubai it is common to celebrate different cultures and to work with people from different countries. During those 2 years I realized that we can learn a lot more by just living in a foreign country, not only by just living but getting involve to groups of students from different countries, for example, I learned about different cultures and most importantly I learned how to adopt to a new environment.

Working as part of group in university made me realize that we can benefit a lot by working together as a team and working with students from different backgrounds makes us learn in so many different ways that we can not imagine. Also, it allowed me to gain awareness and admiration to different cultures. Society needs diversity because monism can not maintain the society and on the other hand as a great example, it is such an important phenomena in evaluation to species have different sets of DNA, evolution praises diversity in genes and we see the same kind of diversity in society by having people from different cultures and countries. Diversity leads to different kinds of mindset and it leads distinct point of views and especially in computer systems we need new and unique ideas and solutions.

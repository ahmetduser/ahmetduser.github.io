Who is Ahmet Duser

July 24, 2020  |  Posted by AhmetDuser

I was born and brought up in Turkey, my father is electronics Engineer and my mother is a housewife. My parents have always laid great importance on Education and both of them are very progressive and modern in their thoughts. It was my father who inspired me to become passionate about Engineering, especially about Computer Systems Engineering. I watched my dad work on different circuits and programs and I always wondered if I would ever become a successful person like my dad. My dad always supported me and the choices I made. I completed my high school in Turkey. After my high school, we moved to UAE because my father got a job as a project manager in a Multinational Technology Company based in Switzerland. I joined Middlesex University (Mdx), where I finished the International Foundation program for Engineering in 2019. After IFP, I completed the year one Undergraduate degree BEng Computer Systems Engineering in Mdx.
